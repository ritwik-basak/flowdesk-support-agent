# Flowdesk Billing and Subscription Guide

This billing guide explains Flowdesk pricing, subscription management, invoices, refunds, and payment-related issues. If your workspace is managed by a company billing admin, some options described below may only be visible to admins or account owners.

## Available pricing plans

Flowdesk offers three plans designed for individuals, growing teams, and larger organizations.

### Free

Best for personal planning or small teams evaluating the platform.

Includes:

- Up to 5 workspace members
- Basic task and project management
- Board and list views
- Limited integrations
- Standard email support

### Pro

Price: `₹799/month` per workspace member

Best for teams that need recurring workflows, reporting, and stronger collaboration tools.

Includes everything in Free, plus:

- Unlimited projects
- Recurring tasks
- Timeline view
- CSV export
- Slack and Google Calendar integrations
- Advanced notifications
- Priority support

### Business

Price: `₹2499/month` per workspace member

Best for larger teams that require controls, automation, and admin oversight.

Includes everything in Pro, plus:

- Advanced permission controls
- Audit logs
- Admin reporting
- Higher automation limits
- Dedicated onboarding assistance
- Faster support response targets

## How to upgrade or downgrade a plan

Only billing admins or workspace owners can change a subscription.

1. Open the relevant workspace.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**.
3. Select **Change Plan**.
4. Review the available plans and feature differences.
5. Choose **Upgrade** or **Downgrade**.
6. Confirm the seat count and billing cycle, if applicable.
7. Save the change.

Upgrades usually take effect immediately. Downgrades may apply at the next renewal date if the current billing period has already started. If your current usage exceeds the limits of the lower plan, you may be asked to reduce members, integrations, or premium features before the downgrade completes.

## How to cancel a subscription

To cancel a paid subscription:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**.
2. Open **Manage Subscription**.
3. Click **Cancel Plan**.
4. Review the cancellation summary.
5. Confirm the cancellation.

After cancellation, your paid features remain available until the end of the current billing period unless otherwise stated during checkout or in a promotional offer. At the end of that period, the workspace typically moves to the Free plan, subject to Free plan limits.

## Refund policy

Flowdesk generally does not provide partial refunds for unused time in the middle of a billing cycle. However, refunds may be considered in limited situations such as:

- Duplicate charges
- Incorrect seat billing caused by a verified platform issue
- Accidental renewal reported shortly after the billing date

Refund requests should be submitted through support with:

- Workspace name
- Billing email
- Invoice number
- Reason for the refund request

Approved refunds are typically returned to the original payment method. Processing time depends on the payment provider and bank.

## Payment methods accepted

Flowdesk accepts common digital payment methods for self-serve subscriptions.

Supported methods may include:

- Major credit cards
- Major debit cards
- UPI-enabled payments in supported regions
- Net banking through supported gateways

Available options can vary by country, currency, and billing profile. Enterprise-style invoicing may be available only on approved Business accounts.

## How to download an invoice

Billing admins can download invoices for completed payments.

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**.
2. Open **Invoices** or **Billing History**.
3. Select the invoice you want.
4. Click **Download PDF**.

Invoices generally include:

- Invoice number
- Billing period
- Workspace name
- Seat count
- Taxes, if applicable
- Payment status

If you need billing details updated for future invoices, edit the billing profile before the next renewal date.

## What happens if payment fails

If a recurring payment fails, Flowdesk attempts to collect payment again automatically using the saved payment method. During this period:

- Billing admins may receive email alerts
- In-app warnings may appear in the billing section
- Paid features remain active for a short grace period

If payment is not resolved after repeated attempts, the workspace may be restricted or downgraded. To avoid interruption:

1. Update the payment method in **Settings** > **Billing**.
2. Confirm the card or bank account is active.
3. Check whether your bank blocked the transaction.
4. Retry the payment from the billing page if the option is available.

## How to apply a coupon code

Coupon or promotional codes can usually be applied during checkout or plan upgrade.

1. Start the checkout or upgrade flow.
2. Locate the **Coupon Code** or **Promo Code** field.
3. Enter the code exactly as provided.
4. Click **Apply**.
5. Review the updated subtotal before completing payment.

Important notes:

- Coupon codes may be limited to first-time upgrades or specific plans.
- Only one coupon code can usually be applied per transaction.
- Expired or region-restricted codes will not validate.

If a valid code does not apply correctly, capture a screenshot of the checkout page and contact support before completing payment.
